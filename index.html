Welcome to Smoked Sofrito

At Smoked Sofrito, we offer authentic Puerto Rican cuisine with a soulful twist. Our food truck is dedicated to bringing people together through delicious and memorable dishes.

Our menu is carefully crafted to offer a variety of options that are sure to satisfy every taste bud. From traditional dishes like arroz con gandules and tostones, to our signature smoked meats, we use only the freshest ingredients and bold flavors.

Visit us at our food truck or book us for your next event. We look forward to serving you!
