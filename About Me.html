About Us

Our Story

Hey there, I'm a 29-year-old guy who's starting a food truck because I love to cook and share my food with others. There's nothing quite like seeing the joy on someone's face when they take that first bite and realize how delicious a dish is. For me, food is about bringing people together and creating memories.

I was raised by a Puerto Rican mother and grandmother who instilled in me a love of authentic food. I grew up with big meals and family gatherings as a staple in my life. I learned how to cook from them and developed a passion for creating dishes that are full of flavor and soul.

Starting a food truck is a dream come true for me because it allows me to share my love of food with even more people. Whether you're craving classic Puerto Rican dishes or want to try something new, my menu is crafted with care to bring you dishes that are both delicious and memorable.

Our Mission

At Smoked Sofrito, our mission is to bring people together through delicious and memorable dishes. We believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a way to connect with others and create lasting memories. That's why we use only the freshest ingredients and bold flavors in our dishes, and why we're dedicated to providing exceptional customer service to every person who visits our food truck.

Our Promise

When you visit Smoked Sofrito, you can expect to be greeted with a smile and treated like family. We promise to always use the highest quality ingredients in our dishes. We are committed to delivering excellent customer service and making sure that each and every one of our customers leaves satisfied and happy. Thank you for choosing Smoked Sofrito, and we look forward to sharing our passion for authentic, delicious food with you.
